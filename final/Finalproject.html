Lambda max values

On the graph above, the lambda max values are marked with a black dot. These wavelengths represent the wavelength at which the solution is emitted the largest amount of photons.

The lambda max values of emission are obtained by looking at a range of detection wavelengths while at one excitation wavelength. The lambda max can also be associated with a color of the visible light spectrum.

The lines in the graph are printed in the color that they correlate to based on its lambda max.
